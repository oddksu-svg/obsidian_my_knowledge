# Main Content Architecture

A 3-column grid system surfacing the most critical institutional data.

## News (Column 1)
- Vertical stack of News Cards.
- Anatomy: Thumbnail, Date, Headline, Excerpt, "Read More".

## Admissions (Column 2)
- Theme: [[Design System#Color Palette|Admissions Green]].
- Links to:
	- Below Bachelor's
	- Undergraduate
	- Postgraduate

## Academic Programs (Column 3)
- List of the 6 core faculties:
	1. Agricultural Technology
	2. Management Sciences
	3. Engineering & Industrial Tech
	4. Science & Health Tech
	5. Liberal Arts
	6. Education & Innovation
